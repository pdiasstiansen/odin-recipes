Roasted Chickpea and broccoli burritos

This is a fan favorite that had to appear in the book. It's a weeknight staple and one bad burrito you deserve to have in your life. Listen to the fans, The know what's up.

Ingredients

  • 1 Large yellow onion
  • 1 red bell pepper
  • 1 large crown o broccoli
  • 3 cups of cooked chickpeas*
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 to 2 tablespoons soy sauce, tamari, or Bragg's**
  • 2 teaspoons chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground coriander***
  • Cayenne pepper, to taste
  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • 1/2 lime
  • 4 to 6 flour tortillas
  • Burrito trimmings such as spinach, avocado, cilantro, and Fire-Roasted Salsa

Steps

  1. Crank your oven to 425 F.
  2. Chop up the onion, bell pepper, and broccoli.
  3. Put all chopped vegies into a large bowl with the chickpeas.
  4. Put in all the oil, soy sauce, and spices and mix.
  5. Bake for 20 minutes.
  6. Remove from oven, mix then bake for 15 minutes.
  7. Remove from oven then squeeze lime juice on top then mix.
  8. Make burritos.
